Gather

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 Tbs bacon fat
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 slice tomato
  • 1/2 avocado
  • Non-stick pan

Grilled Cheese

Step 1

Place non-stick pan on stovetop over med-high heat. Once hot, place 1 Tbs butter in pan. When butter begins to sizzle, place both slices of bread in pan.

Grilled Cheese

Step 2

Swish bread around in pan to ensure butter coats bread. Fry until bottom of bread is golden brown. Flip bread slices over.

Grilled Cheese

Step 3

Add both slices of cheese to one piece of the bread. Smear other piece of bread with 1/2 avocado. Place tomato on top of avocado.

Grilled Cheese

Step 4

Once bottom of bread is golden brown, remove from heat and put both pieces of bread together. Enjoy!
